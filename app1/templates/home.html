About Us

Hire-U is a Kerala Government-undertaking that focuses on skilling students and the general public to enhance employability. Kerala has designed an exclusive industry-led skill training ecosystem through Skill Development Centres. It connects higher secondary schools, polytechnic institutes, and arts & science and engineering colleges to 16 Community Skill Parks. ASAP Kerala has 12 NSQF Skill Qualifications registered in the National Qualifications Register (NQR). Preparing youth for a rapidly evolving future through state-of-the-art training programmes
in highly demanding areas is the objective of ASAP Kerala. ASAP Kerala has made a positive impact on raising the employability of the State's manpower.
